Georg and his Pumkin latte

George recomends our pumpkin spice lattes with fresh local produce. Instead of using machine harvested pumpkins, we use ours due to the amount of juice that overflows from the pumkins to be mixed into the latte.

Ingredients 1: Pumpkin milk 2: espresso

The ingredient list is pretty simple. Using real pumkin juice allows the flavor to come alive in the latte.
